SECTION: Overview of Fees
American Airlines charges fees for optional services and checked bags on a per-person basis, per direction, even when upgraded or holding elite status. All bag fees are non-refundable when paid at the airport. If you believe you were incorrectly charged, contact customer service or file a claim within 45 days. :contentReference[oaicite:0]{index=0}

SECTION: Fee Structure
Fees vary by:
- Fare type
- Travel region
- Timing of payment (online vs airport)
- Passenger’s elite status or card benefits
Different fees may apply for oversize, overweight, or additional bags. :contentReference[oaicite:1]{index=1}

SECTION: Refund Claims
Passengers may request refund claims or support for optional service fees through American’s support channels if charged incorrectly. Fee disputes generally require contacting customer support or filing a form. :contentReference[oaicite:2]{index=2}
