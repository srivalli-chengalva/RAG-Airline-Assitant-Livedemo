SECTION: Ancillary Refunds
American handles refunds for inflight purchase fees, Wi-Fi, and other services per internal procedures; passengers may contact customer service or use the online refund status tool. :contentReference[oaicite:18]{index=18}

SECTION: Ticket Type Considerations
Refund eligibility depends on ticket type (refundable vs non-refundable), timing of request, third-party purchase, and airline’s own fare conditions. :contentReference[oaicite:19]{index=19}
