SECTION: Fee Structure by Region
Checked bag fees for flights as of December 1, 2025:
- Within/Between U.S., Puerto Rico, U.S. Virgin Islands:
  • 1st checked bag fee $40 ($35 if paid online)
  • 2nd checked bag fee $45 ($45 if paid online)
- Canada / Caribbean / Mexico / Central America / Guyana:
  • Same fee structure applies
Fees are per passenger, per direction, and separate from ticket costs. :contentReference[oaicite:4]{index=4}

SECTION: Bag Allowance & Fees
Checked baggage fees apply per bag per direction even if multiple bags are checked on a single reservation. Online pre-payment generally offers lower fees than paying at the airport. :contentReference[oaicite:5]{index=5}

SECTION: Refund Right
If American does not carry over checked bags to a new flight after a change, the fees may be refunded as travel credit or claimable through support. :contentReference[oaicite:6]{index=6}
